A Chat section is designed to enable real-time conversations between the community members.

A Posts type section is where you can put individual posts and other users can comment or upvote.


This type of section is most beneficial for:


Organizing important conversation topics

Publishing content you want users to notice 5

A Group is a space where you can add multiple sections, depending on what you want to use these sections for.


Groups can be:


Open and Free For Everyone - Anyone with your community URL will be able to see the sections and content inside them.

Closed and Free For Everyone - Users will be able to Request Access to this group. This Request needs to be approved by the Community Admin.

Paid Group - Community Admin can decide a price tag for access to the group. Users have to first make payments to access such groups.
